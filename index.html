Los Frijolitos

Los Frijolitos del Peru is a peruvian restaurant with locations in Miami, United States, born to spread our gastronomy Costa, Sierra and Selva abroad. Our menu includes traditional Creole cuisine, with classic style Aromas of Peru, as well as current innovations while respecting the classic.
